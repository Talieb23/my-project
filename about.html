Bio


When I was little I adored puzzles, riddles, and anything that involved creative problem solving. That passion
helped me to find my first love of Aviation. I have been an Air Traffic Controller for the past 22 years. Controlling
the busy and complex traffic of Boston Logan Airport has assisted in improving my mental acuity, task switching skills,
and my ability to "think outside the box". This, in tandem with problem solving, quick reaction time, and persistence are
the key skills needed to peform in todays complex and technology driven world.

I have found that all of these transition well to the field of web development. The added element of creativity has
ignited a passion in me that I have not felt since my first day "talking to airplanes". That feeling of excitement around
learning something new and diving in "head first" has been wonderful! I cannot wait to see where this journey takes me!!
